About Me

I received my PhD from University of North Carolina at Charlotte, USA in 2012 in the field of Mobile Ad hoc and Sensor Networks, Communication Protocols, and Simulations. My research work focuses mostly on communication and protocol evaluation, Blockchain technologies, Information Centric Networking, Internet of Things, Software-defined networking, 5G/6G systems, and Simulation for mobile and wireless ad hoc/sensor networks. Currently I am working as Associate Professor (Research) at Beijing Institute of Technology, Beijing, China. I am a senior member of IEEE and Associate Editor of IEEE Access.
