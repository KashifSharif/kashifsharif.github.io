Disclaimer:

All info is based on China Southern Flight Isb-Wuhan on Feb 1 2021. Policies change on daily basis, so this is only a guideline. Source of info is other wechat groups and my HR's advice.

Last updated: Jan 26, 2021

Upcoming Process

  • Editor: Go to Embassy on Jan 30, Saturday, at 1300. The sample collection at Embassy is between 1300 and 1800 hrs.
  • Editor: Two different labs (at the embassy) will be used for taking samples. Sample for Nucleic Acid and IgM anti-body will be collected (IgG included). In person and with your passport. Cost per lab is approx 10,000 Rs. So, multiply by 2.
  • Editor: When the results comes back (SMS from lab), apply for "HDC" on-line: http://hrhk.cs.mfa.gov.cn/H5/. If some documents are missing/needed code color will be red. (Visa, passport, reports etc). Make sure to upload everything. New advisory requires IgM value to be below 0.5 to apply for HDC. Travelers with children, need to get the HDC for them also.
  • Editor: Print the HDC. Save a Pic of it on your phone also. (It should be a GREEN QR code). This has a validity period. It should not be a problem with this group, but be aware of it.
  • Editor: Bring it with you for boarding the flight.

HDC Application:

  • Editor: You can create an account on http://hrhk.cs.mfa.gov.cn/H5/
  • Editor: It can be done in advance. It only requires a valid email address. Website is in English and Mandarin.
  • Editor: To create, enter your email, and a verification code EMAIL will come (Check Spam folder if it does not come in few minutes). Enter it, and fill the security questions.
  • Editor: You can use this account to file for "Health Declaration Code" after getting the results.
  • Editor: Same login can be used for your spouse and children. You will see options to apply for yourself and for 'other persons'.
  • Editor: For applying, you need your flight info, passport info, and test result info. Also a phone number (mobile) will be required. Then also upload the required pics/documents.
  • Editor: Wait for the Green code to come. (I am not sure how long it will take for the HDC to come back).

What if the results are bad?

  • Editor: How bad are they? 1- PCR positive: Go to a hospital. You have Corona. 2- IgM above normal: You have recently (with last few days) been exposed to virus. Quarantine yourself and get tested again in a few days. Any symptoms show up, go to a hospital. 3- IgG above normal: You may have been exposed to virus a while ago. Wait some more time. Take vitamins, stay healthy.
  • Editor: Do not apply for HDC if you know your results are not in range. Someone shared a "HDC account blocked" picture with a RED code.
  • Editor:Go to China Southern website/app and change your ticket. Refund or change to a future date (if possible). CAAC's new advisory on Full Ticket Refunds in not applicable for this.
  • Editor: This is why it is very important to social distance at the embassy. You don't want to be tested positive in China because of a recent exposure.

What to expect in Wuhan:

  • Editor: You will be taken for isolation in a predefined place.
  • Editor: Cost is around 6000 RMB or higher. You don't get to chose where to go.
  • Editor: Halal food is included in this. Don't expect biryani.
  • Editor: Ordering cooked food is not allowed. If anything changes in this regard, then enjoy.
  • Editor: Others have told that fruits and some other (non-cooked) things can be bought using a QR code in the room.

Important (Holiday Season):

  • Editor: Wuhan is currently "Low-Risk Area". Pray it stays that way.
  • Editor: Currently 14+7 quarantine is active.
  • Editor: This means 14 days of isolation in a centralized predefined location is must. +7 days may or may not happen. Depends on situation.
  • Editor: +7 more in isolation, if anyone on the flight is tested positive in China. All potentially exposed will have to quarantine for +7 (at least).
  • Editor: Local authorities may dictate a +7 due to holiday travel or new cases in city or reports of increase in country.
  • Editor: If your final destination is Wuhan, +7 home quarantine MAY BE allowed.
  • Editor: Wuhan is "Low Risk", which means, that if you will go to another city, you will quarantine 14 days at home there. Be aware of your destination city regulations.
  • Editor: Check if your residential community should be aware in advance of your arrival. Due to holiday season, rules are more strict.
  • Editor: Check your destination city and area. If it has been declared Mid-Risk or High-Risk don't go there.

Applying for Residency Permits (Holiday Season):

  • Editor: If you are traveling on Z or S visa (000 entry), you will be applying for residency permit. You know the process.
  • Editor: Have your HR prepare your documents NOW (if you need anything from them). They will be on holiday, when your quarantine ends. Or make sure, they will be in office when you are arriving in your city.
  • Editor: If you are in quarantine for 14 Wuhan + 14 in your city, your window to apply within 30 days will be very small. Make sure your documents are ready. I am not aware if anyone has experienced any relaxation in 30 day limit.

Health Apps:

  • Editor: Every city in China has a health app now. It is used to keep track of your health status. It will give you a GREEN code if you are OK to move around. You have to scan the QR at almost all major places.
  • Editor: You can try to get it in advance for your city, and register via wechat. It will ask for passport number and probably a picture of it too.
  • Editor: It also shows the result of your COVID tests in China.
