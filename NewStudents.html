New MS/PhD Student Requirements and Instructions

Applicants must ensure that they are willing to pursue an MS or PhD program in the School of Computer Science and Technology, at Beijing Institute of Technology, for the year 2024/25.

The research focus must be on one of the research areas listed on this webpage. The PhD students must publish 2 journal papers during the studies (of which 1 must be in Q1 journal), in addition to a complete thesis. For MS students 1 publication in an SCI journal is sufficient.

To request a recommendation/acceptance letter and discuss the details, email the following information. This does not guarantee a recommendation or admission.

Following Requirements are in addition to the university requirements for admission application.

Deadline to apply is Nov. 15th, 2023.

PhD Applicants

  • Student should have completed MS degree in past 2 years, or should be in final year.
  • First Page of Passport
  • IELTS/TOEFL Score (Preferred)
  • Research Statement
  • Personal Statement
  • BS Transcript
  • MS Transcript
  • MS Thesis Document (PDF)
  • 2 x reference letters (Must describe your potential as a PhD candidate. Generic letters will be ignored immediately.)
  • Publications (Not mandatory, but will be given preference)
  • Link to Linkedin Profile

MS Applicants

  • Student should have completed BS degree in past 1 years, or should be in final year.
  • First Page of Passport
  • Personal Statement
  • 1 page essay on research interest
  • BS Transcript
  • 1 x reference letter
  • Link to Linkedin Profile

Research Areas

  • Computer Networks
  • Wireless and Mobile Computing
  • Blockchain and DLTs
  • Software-defined Networks
  • Data/Information Centric Networks
  • 5G and beyond, Tactile Internet
  • Vehicular and UAV Networks
  • Network Security
  • Internet of Things
